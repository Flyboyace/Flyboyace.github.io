Elemental tower defense

Overview

Elemental Tower Defense is my first tower defense game, where you need to defend your base against skeleton enemies with your elemental towers. Such as a fire tower that fires rapidly or an ice tower that slows the enemy down.

Reflection

This was my first tower defense project that I created at school with help from classmates and AI. I struggled most with starting scripts and structuring my code properly. It was also difficult to make my game good replayability wise.

What I Used

Features

• Wave system
• Music
